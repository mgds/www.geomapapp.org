Dear GeoMapApp community,

The developers of GeoMapApp would like to hear from you.

We would like to expand the data-browsing content in GeoMapApp and Virtual Ocean. Is there Public Domain content you would like to see added to that already available in GeoMapApp and Virtual Ocean?

This content could be in the form of maps, grids, images, tables or spreadsheets.

Please send a link to the data and a brief description of the source and content. Email your suggestions to us at info@geomapapp.org.

As always, you may email us with any problems, comments, and/or suggestions: info@geomapapp.org
