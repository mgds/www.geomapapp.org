When You Visit Our Website or Use GeoMapApp

We do not collect personally identifiable information (for example, name, address, phone number, email address) from users, regardless of age, unless you provide it to us.

Usage Statistics

As part of standard internet practice, we collect standard web log information such as IP address, date/time of access, type of browser and operating system when you visit our site. We use IP addresses and other web log information to analyze trends, track usage of system features, tools and datasets, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use for reporting and sponsorship purposes. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing

We will share aggregated demographic information with our sponsors. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. We only share this information so that we can secure funding to continue providing this site free of charge.

Links

This Website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we can not claim any responsibility for the privacy practices of such other sites. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Website.

Email Sent to our Help Desk

When you send an electronic mail message to our Help Desk (info[at]geomapapp.org), you may be sending personal information. In these cases, we retain the information only to respond to your request. We do not share this information with any other organization or entity.

Newsletter

If you sign up for our eNewsletter we may contact you to send you newsletters, service updates, and occasional requests for user surveys. If you wish to unsubscribe, please follow the instructions here.
